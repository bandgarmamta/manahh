About us

The App would enable the students to take Psychological tests recommended for recruitment and selection processes. The proposed App development aims at spreading awareness and support about numerous Mental Health issues. The App would provide factual and authentic Mental health information to the students.

Helping students identify their potential and area of improvements before going for placements. Most available apps use the traditional way to take psychological tests that is by asking a bunch of questions to the users which makes the overall process boring and less interactive but we are trying to propose a different way of taking these tests such that the users find it engaging and helpful at the same time.
